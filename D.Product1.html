DJI Mini 2 SE Drone

The DJI Mini 2 SE is practically as light as your smartphone. So, not only can you stow it away in a backpack without any fuss, it's also light enough to be exempt from certain regulations in the UK (and many other countries). So why not go long haul? It can fly and record video 6 miles away for some truly epic views. A flight time of up to 31 minutes gives you plenty of chances to nail those aerial shots.
