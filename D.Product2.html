DJI Mini 3 Drone

Weighing in at just under 250g, the DJI Mini 3 Drone is almost as light as the average smartphone. It's small enough to pop in your bag and take with you anywhere. And better still, the lightweight design places it in the lowest and safest weight class of drones, so it's exempt from certain regulations in the UK and many other countries. You can say goodbye to battery anxiety too. The Intelligent Flight Battery will get the Mini 3 in the air for up to 38 minutes (and you get 3 batteries in this combo). And the best bit? You can capture the view in 4K HDR video that's packed with life-like colours and plenty of details.
