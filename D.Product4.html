DJI Air 3 Drone

Capture more with the DJI Air 3 and its dual cameras. For dramatic landscapes, go wide with an epic 24 mm f/1.7 wide-angle. To frame your subject, get closer with the 70 mm f/2.8 medium telephoto. Both cameras are equipped with a 1/1.3” CMOS sensor, dishing out crisp 48-megapixel images ready for tight cropping. It also outputs 4K 60 fps HDR video without cropping. That means footage is brimming with vibrant colours and smooth lighting transitions. And with an impressive 46 minutes flight time (that's nearly twice as much as the Air 2S), you've got all the time you need to nail that perfect shot. No rush, no fuss!
