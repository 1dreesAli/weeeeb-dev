DJI Mini 2 SE Drone

The DJI Mini 2 SE is practically as light as your smartphone. So, not only can you stow it away in a backpack without any fuss, it's also light enough to be exempt from certain regulations in the UK (and many other countries). So why not go long haul? It can fly and record video 6 miles away for some truly epic views. A flight time of up to 31 minutes gives you plenty of chances to nail those aerial shots.

DJI Mini 3 Drone

Weighing in at just under 250g, the DJI Mini 3 Drone is almost as light as the average smartphone. It's small enough to pop in your bag and take with you anywhere. And better still, the lightweight design places it in the lowest and safest weight class of drones, so it's exempt from certain regulations in the UK and many other countries.

DJI Mini 4 Pro Drone

At under 249 g, the DJI Mini 4 Pro is nearly as light as your smartphone. That makes it exempt from certain UK regulations – and portable enough to fit in pretty much any bag. Take to the skies and capture the view with 4K HDR video at 60 fps.
