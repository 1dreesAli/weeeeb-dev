DJI Mavic 3 Classic Drone

DJI have partnered with the legendary camera maker Hasselblad for the Mavic 3 Classic drone. Just like it's Mavic 3 sibling, the Classic can shoot 5.1K resolution footage and 20-megapixel images thanks to the large 4/3 CMOS sensor. Or play around and record at 4K at 120 fps for some slow-mo fun. In Full HD it can go up to 200 fps, for even greater freedom in the editing suite. You'll be able to capture some epic landscapes with the 24 mm equivalent focal length lens. The Hasselblad Natural Colour Solution (HNCS) makes sure recorded colours accurately match the scene you're filming. No need for colour correction while editing – it'll look stunning right off the camera.
