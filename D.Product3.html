DJI Mini 4 Pro Drone

At under 249 g, the DJI Mini 4 Pro is nearly as light as your smartphone. That makes it exempt from certain UK regulations – and portable enough to fit in pretty much any bag. Take to the skies and capture the view with 4K HDR video at 60 fps. Landscapes will be packed with detail and rich colours, while night views on are par with the Air 3 thanks to improved processing and a f/1.7 aperture. Grab cinematic moments with Mastershots – simply select a manoeuvre and the drone will perform it, all while keeping you in frame. Or slow things down with 4K 100 fps slow motion – the high frame rate makes for super smooth footage.
